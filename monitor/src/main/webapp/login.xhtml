Welcome to DataCleaner dq monitor

What is it?

Monitor the evolution of your data

DataCleaner dq monitor helps your visualize and explore the evolution of your data. Use it to surveil your data activities and manage your data’s quality.

Share your data analysis with everyone

Use DataCleaner to explore, profile and analyze your data. Use the monitor webapplication to share it with anyone, even to set up an automatic analysis at regular intervals.

Continuously improve your data’s quality

DataCleaner dq monitor orchestrates and schedules DataCleaner jobs, used to cleanse and enrich your data.

Connect DataCleaner to the world

With the dq monitor you can securely connect DataCleaner jobs to other business processes using web services, scheduled executions and more. Instantly use your jobs for both batch and real-time processing of data.
