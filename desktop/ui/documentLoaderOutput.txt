	<h1>Pattern finder</h1> 

	<h4>Description : The Pattern Finder will inspect your String values and generate and match string patterns that suit your data.
It can be used for a lot of purposes but is excellent for verifying or getting ideas about the format of the string-values in a column. </h4>
